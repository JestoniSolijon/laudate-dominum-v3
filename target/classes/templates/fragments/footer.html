Refund Policy

Our policy lasts 7 days. If 7 days have gone by since you received the item, unfortunately we can’t offer you a refund or exchange. It must also be in the original packaging.

To request for return you may email us at mao.officialgroup@gmail.com or for immediate response message us on our facebook page facebook.com/laudatedominumofficial. Please attach photos and description of the damaged product.
